## Important Design Decisions to Preserve

- The Health Science strand is normative, not neutral. It explicitly connects healthy eating and exercise to improved appearance, energy, and cognitive performance. It is judgmental about ignoring science. See Section 1.2 ("The Standard: Building the Best Version of Yourself").
- Module H8 (Dopamine, Screens, and Sustained Attention) is especially important — the student measures their own attentional impairment from short-form video consumption. This is the highest-leverage module for an ADHD learner.
- H3 (Protein and Diet Planning) teaches practical macro-nutrient principles. H7 (Strength Training) includes a 3-week training program with baseline/retest measurements. AH5 (Building a Personal Protocol) is the capstone where the student designs their own science-based daily plan.
- The strand follows the same design principles as everything else: experiment-first, precise terminology at point of observation, method sidebars, research reports, glossary entries, challenge mode.
- All existing modules (P, C, B, E series) are unchanged. The Health Science strand adds to the curriculum; it doesn't modify existing content.
