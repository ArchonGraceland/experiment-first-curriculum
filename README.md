# Experiment-First Science Curriculum

A high school science curriculum (Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Earth & Space Science, Health Science) that teaches through experiments instead of definitions. Designed for a 16-year-old with ADHD.

**Project hub:** [https://archongraceland.github.io/experiment-first-curriculum/](https://archongraceland.github.io/experiment-first-curriculum/)

## Curriculum Overview

- **Core:** 61 modules (~31 weeks) across 5 domains (4 NGSS + Health Science)
- **Advanced:** 19 modules (~10 weeks) for deeper exploration
- **Total:** 80 modules, ~40 weeks across 2 years, ~2 hours/week
- **Health Science strand:** 18 modules — self-improvement through science (nutrition, exercise, sleep, screens, skin, stress, personal protocol)
